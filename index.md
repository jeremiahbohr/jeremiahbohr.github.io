Jeremiah Bohr is an Assistant Professor of Sociology at the University of Wisconsin Oshkosh.  His research interests include analyzing climate change denial, environmental communication and politics, and household energy use.  He uses computational methods (especially text analysis) as well as traditional survey methods and content analysis.
